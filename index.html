Workflow

A clean handoff from intake to insight to action.

The system is designed like a care operations layer: fast to scan, easy to trust, and structured around the real sequence diagnostic teams already follow.

1

Ingest the case

Bring in the image set, labs, vitals, referral context, and prior records as soon as the case arrives.

2

Dispatch the right agents

Assign specialized agents to triage urgency, inspect the modality, summarize evidence, and flag uncertainty.

3

Surface the rationale

Show what drove the recommendation, what information was missing, and where a clinician should focus next.

4

Move the case forward

Send the case to the right queue, request follow-up, or complete review with the final decision attached.
